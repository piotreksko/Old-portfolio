About me

My name is Piotr Skorupa and I'm a self-taught web developer. I have finished a Front-end developer bootcamp on FreeCodeCamp where I have completed many challenges and became familiar with HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript and the use of libraries such as Bootstrap and jQuery. After finishing the bootcamp I am motivated to develop my skills related to Front-end development and learn something new everyday. My next goal is to be more fluent in JavaScript and learn React.

For me the most interesting thing about developing websites is the combination of creative design and never-ending challenge of figuring out a way to make things work as intended.

My English level is advanced both written and spoken and I use this language almost everyday whether it's watching a movie or reading a book.
