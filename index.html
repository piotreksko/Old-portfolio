About me

My name is Piotr Skorupa and I'm a self-taught web developer. I have finished a Front-end developer bootcamp on FreeCodeCamp where I have completed many challenges and became familiar with HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript and the use of libraries such as Bootstrap and jQuery. My English level is very advanced both written and spoken and I use this language almost everyday whether it's watching a movie or reading a book.
